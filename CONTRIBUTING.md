## ğŸ“Š Resource Quality Criteria

Please ensure the resource:

* Has **clear educational or practical value**
* Comes from a **credible author, institution, or organization**
* Is **accessible** (clearly mark if paid, free, or requires signup)
* Uses an **appropriate difficulty label** (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)
* Respects **copyright and licensing**

---

## ğŸ“„ Special Guidelines

### Research Papers

* Prefer peer-reviewed or widely cited papers
* Include a short summary of the contribution
* Link to arXiv or the official publisher

### Tools & Libraries

* Must be actively maintained
* Include supported frameworks (PyTorch, TensorFlow, etc.)
* Avoid unmaintained or abandoned projects

### Datasets

* Clearly describe the task and domain
* Mention license and usage restrictions
* Avoid private or inaccessible datasets
