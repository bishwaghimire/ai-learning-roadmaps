# Research Scientist Roadmap (PhD-Level AI & ML)

This roadmap guides learners from **advanced theoretical foundations** to **independent research**, **paper writing**, and **frontier-level contributions** in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.  
By the end, you should be able to **formulate research questions, design experiments, publish papers, and push the boundaries of AI**.

> A **complete Research Scientist roadmap** — from mathematical rigor to original AI research.

## Prerequisites

Before starting this roadmap, you should already have:

- **Strong Mathematics**
  - Linear Algebra
  - Probability Theory
  - Statistics
  - Optimization

- **Advanced ML / DL**
  - CNNs, RNNs, Transformers
  - Optimization & Generalization
  - Representation Learning

- **Programming**
  - Python (PyTorch / JAX)
  - Research code hygiene

- **Research Mindset**
  - Curiosity
  - Patience
  - Comfort with ambiguity

- **Method Improvement / Ablation**
  *Skills:* Hypothesis Testing

  *Skills:* Scientific Rigor
